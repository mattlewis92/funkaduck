  F U N K A D U C K   D I S C O


Funkaduck is a professional mobile disco based at Tavistock on the Devon & Cornwall border.
We are the areas most experienced wedding & party disco, highly suitable for all functions and events, big or small.
If you need a great disco-show call us now.



Choosing the disco for your special event can be very difficult, especially when planning your wedding.
Email or call us now, we will be pleased assist you and answer your questions.



Funkaduck is our modern digital disco. Our show uses the latest LED lighting & excellent sound equipment. Our disco team is experienced in all function and have a huge music selection which covers the current charts and all of the decades.

Formed in 1970 the original Formula One Disco was replaced by the Paul King Disco Roadshow and now, fronted by Sam King with Paul in the passenger seat the all new Funkaduck Disco. As seen in many top venues, Funkaduck is a great disco for your special occasion.



Funkaduck Disco is suitable for all function sizes.

Our wedding disco is the most popular package and is the ideal size for most hotels & function rooms.

We can reduce the disco size for smaller venues & home parties, or we can enlarge it for bigger events.
